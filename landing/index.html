Features

Everything you need to write

All the tools professional screenwriters expect, built into one free application.

Industry-Standard Editor

Scene headings, action, character, dialogue, parenthetical, transitions, and shots with proper formatting.

Beat Board & Index Cards

Visual story planning with drag-and-drop scene organization. Structure your story before you write.

Real-Time Collaboration

Write together with live cursors, presence indicators, and granular permissions.

Version History

Built-in version control with check-in, side-by-side diff, and instant restore of any draft.

Character Profiles

Track characters with descriptions, role types, and color-coded highlighting throughout your script.

Project Management

Organize multiple screenplays with metadata, assets, and research notes.

Search & Replace

Find and replace across your screenplay. Jump between scenes with the navigator.

Import & Export

Work with PDF, Final Draft FDX, and Fountain. Your scripts are never locked in.

Distraction-Free Mode

Hide the menu and toolbar for a clean, full-screen writing experience. Just you and your screenplay.

Privacy-First

Zero tracking, zero analytics, zero data collection. Works 100% offline.

Your Script. Your Machine. Your IP.

Your intellectual property stays yours

Most screenwriting software sends your scripts to someone else's servers. OpenDraft doesn't.

100% Local Storage

Every screenplay is stored in a local database on your device. No cloud sync. No vendor servers. No third-party access.

No Data Mining

Cloud tools can read and train AI on your work. OpenDraft has zero telemetry, zero analytics, zero collection.

Protect Your IP

Your screenplay is your intellectual property. No corporation can mine, monetize, or train models on your creative work.

Open Source Guarantee

Don't take our word for it — read the code yourself. Every line is publicly auditable. No hidden calls, no backdoors.

Other tools store your scripts on their servers. They can change terms, scan for AI training, or shut down. With OpenDraft, your screenplay exists only on your computer, under your control.
