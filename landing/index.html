Features

Everything you need to write

All the tools professional screenwriters expect, built into one free application.

✎

Industry-Standard Editor

Scene headings, action, character, dialogue, parenthetical, transitions, and shots with proper screenplay formatting.

🎬

Beat Board & Index Cards

Visual story planning with drag-and-drop scene organization. Structure your story before you write.

👥

Real-Time Collaboration

Write together with live cursors, presence indicators, and granular permissions. Invite co-writers with a link.

📄

Version History

Built-in version control with check-in, side-by-side diff, and instant restore of any previous draft.

👤

Character Profiles

Track characters with descriptions, role types, images, and color-coded highlighting throughout your script.

📂

Project Management

Organize multiple screenplays with metadata, attached assets, reference documents, and research notes.

🔍

Search & Replace

Find and replace across your entire screenplay. Jump between scenes instantly with the scene navigator.

📦

Import & Export

Work with standard formats including PDF, Final Draft FDX, and Fountain. Your scripts are never locked in.

🔒

Privacy-First

Zero tracking, zero analytics, zero data collection. Works 100% offline. Your scripts stay on your machine.

Your Script. Your Machine. Your IP.

Your intellectual property stays yours

Most screenwriting software sends your scripts to someone else's servers. OpenDraft doesn't. Your words never leave your machine.

🔒

100% Local Storage

Every screenplay you write is stored in a local SQLite database on your own device. No cloud sync. No vendor servers. No third-party access to your scripts, ever.

🚫

No Data Mining

Cloud-based tools can read, analyze, and train AI on your screenplays. OpenDraft has zero telemetry, zero analytics, zero data collection. We can't read your work because we never see it.

🛡

Protect Your IP

Your screenplay is your intellectual property. When you write in OpenDraft, no corporation has access to mine, monetize, or train models on your creative work.

✅

Open Source Guarantee

Don't take our word for it — read the code yourself. OpenDraft is MIT licensed. Every line is publicly auditable. There are no hidden network calls, no tracking pixels, no backdoors.

Other tools store your scripts on their servers. They can change their terms of service at any time. They can scan your work for AI training. They can shut down and take your data with them. With OpenDraft, your screenplay exists only where you put it — on your computer, under your control.
