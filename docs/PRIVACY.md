# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 29, 2026**

## Overview

OpenDraft is a desktop screenwriting application developed by Proteus Technologies Private Limited. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**OpenDraft does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** The application runs entirely on your device.

Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — We do not use any analytics services, crash reporters, or tracking tools.
- **No network connections** — OpenDraft does not connect to any external servers. All functionality works completely offline.
- **No user accounts** — No registration, login, or authentication is required.
- **No cloud storage** — All your screenplays and project data are stored locally on your computer in your user directory.

## Data Storage

Your screenplays, projects, and settings are stored locally on your Mac at:

```
~/Library/Application Support/com.proteus.opendraft.desktop/
```

This data never leaves your device. You can delete it at any time by removing the application and this folder.

## Third-Party Services

OpenDraft does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

OpenDraft does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes here. Since OpenDraft does not collect data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
